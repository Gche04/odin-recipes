Figgy Pudding

Description

Dense, moist cake reminiscent of the Victorian dessert

Ingredients

  • brown sugar
  • egg
  • breadcrumbs
  • spices
  • dried fruits
  • brandy

Steps

  1. Gently heat buttermilk and figs in a saucepan over medium-low heat until softened, 10 to 15 minutes; set aside until cool.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a tube pan.
  3. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, nutmeg, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until firm and pulling away from sides of the pan, about 2 hours. Set aside to cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan..
